Incede has partnered with other technology providers to support enterprise deployments of IBM's Granite models on the watsonx platform, enabling organizations to build hybrid AI systems that combine the advanced capabilities of Granite with the robust infrastructure of Watson. This collaboration focuses on integrating Granite’s open-source foundation models—optimized for tasks like code generation, reasoning, and multilingual processing—into watsonx.ai flows and enterprise environments. The partnership allows clients to leverage Granite for high-performance inference while using Watson for orchestration, compliance, and scalability. These joint efforts are part of IBM’s broader strategy to expand watsonx through ecosystem partnerships, making generative AI more accessible and production-ready for businesses across industries.
